Payment Policy

At Mashtal, we offer safe and secure payment methods for your convenience.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, etc.)
  • Debit Cards
  • Cash on Delivery (for selected areas)
  • Mobile Wallets and Bank Transfers

Payment Security

All payments are processed through secure, encrypted gateways. We do not store your card information.

Issues with Payment

If your payment fails, please check your card balance or try another method. Contact support for further assistance.
